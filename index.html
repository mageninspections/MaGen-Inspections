What Is Radon?

Radon is a radioactive gas that forms naturally in the soil and seeps into homes through foundation cracks and gaps. It's invisible, odorless, and tasteless — the only way to know your level is to test for it.

Learn About Testing
⚠
Radon is the #2 cause of lung cancer in the U.S.

Responsible for approximately 21,000 deaths annually, according to the EPA. Long-term exposure to elevated levels is a serious health risk.

How Radon Enters Your Home

  • Cracks in solid floors and walls
  • Construction joints between wall and floor
  • Gaps around service pipes
  • Cavities inside walls
  • Sump pits and drain openings
