About MaGen Radon Mitigation

A local, owner-operated business built around one purpose: protecting Central Ohio families from radon.

Who We Are

Expert Service. Personal Accountability.

Proudly family-owned and operated in Columbus, Ohio, MaGen Property Inspections and Radon Mitigation specializes in professional radon testing, radon inspection, and radon mitigation services. As a local family business led by Maryan Gelyk, we deliver accurate radon assessments and effective mitigation systems to protect your home and family's health.

Whether you're buying or selling a property, building new, or simply concerned about radon levels in your home, our certified team provides thorough reports and custom mitigation solutions tailored for Central Ohio homes. Serving Columbus and all surrounding areas with the honest, personal care only a family-run company can offer.

Every installation is backed by a personal results guarantee. If levels aren't where they need to be after the job, we come back — at no charge.
